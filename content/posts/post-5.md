Freelancing is great, but it still requires you to work hours for every dollar. Passive income allows you to break free from the hourly trap. Here are three ways to start.

### 1. Build a SaaS Product
Identify a small problem and build a software solution (SaaS). Even a simple tool with a monthly subscription can provide a steady stream of income.

### 2. Create Digital Products
Templates, UI kits, and e-books are high-margin assets. Once created, they can be sold thousands of times on platforms like Gumroad or ThemeForest.

### 3. Start a Technical Blog or YouTube Channel
Sharing your knowledge can lead to ad revenue, sponsorships, and affiliate marketing. It takes time to build an audience, but the long-term rewards are massive.

> "If you don't find a way to make money while you sleep, you will work until you die."
